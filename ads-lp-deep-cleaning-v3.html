Is Deep Cleaning Right for You?

Deep cleaning is a detailed reset, not routine weekly maintenance.

Deep cleaning is perfect if:

  • Your home has not been professionally cleaned in months
  • You are preparing for recurring cleaning
  • You need renovation dust or buildup handled
  • You want a seasonal refresh
  • You are moving into an occupied home that needs extra detail

Consider a different service if:

  • You want weekly upkeep: regular cleaning
  • You are moving out of an empty home: move cleaning
  • You only need a small touch-up: pricing options

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does deep cleaning take?

Most deep cleanings take several hours depending on home size, condition, and add-ons. We confirm timing before the appointment.

What is the difference between regular and deep cleaning?

Regular cleaning maintains a home already in good shape. Deep cleaning adds extra detail for buildup, baseboards, corners, bathroom tile, kitchen grime, and reset-level work.

Do I need to be home?

No. Many clients leave access instructions. We can text updates and confirm when the cleaning is complete.

Can I switch to regular cleaning after?

Yes. Deep cleaning is a great first visit before weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly service.
