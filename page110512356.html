Commercial Cleaning Services

Professional office and business cleaning that keeps your workspace spotless and productive

What's Included in Commercial Cleaning

Comprehensive cleaning for offices, retail spaces, and small businesses

Office Areas & Workspaces

  • Desks and work surfaces wiped and disinfected
  • Computer monitors and keyboards dusted
  • Office equipment cleaned
  • Shelving and filing cabinets dusted
  • Phones and high-touch surfaces sanitized
  • Floors vacuumed and mopped

Break Rooms & Kitchens

  • Countertops wiped and disinfected
  • Sinks scrubbed and sanitized
  • Appliance exteriors cleaned (microwave, fridge, coffee makers)
  • Microwave cleaned inside
  • Tables and chairs wiped
  • Floor swept and mopped

Restrooms

  • Toilets, urinals, sinks fully cleaned and disinfected
  • Mirrors and glass polished
  • Fixtures shined
  • Soap and paper towel dispensers refilled (supplies provided by client)
  • Partitions and walls spot-cleaned
  • Floors mopped and sanitized

Common Areas & Entryways

  • Reception area cleaned and organized
  • Lobby floors vacuumed or mopped
  • Door handles and light switches disinfected
  • Glass doors cleaned
  • Waiting area furniture dusted
  • Trash receptacles emptied
  • High-traffic areas sanitized
