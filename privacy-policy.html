Our Terms of Service ("Terms") govern the Services and contractual matters (scope, payments, cancellations, and dispute procedures). This Privacy Policy governs our Personal Information practices. 2) Personal Information We Collect

A. Information you provide directly
- Contact details: name, email address, phone number (if provided), billing address (if applicable).
- Service details: service address, property type, rooms/bathrooms, condition/soil level, special requests, scheduling details, parking/building requirements.
- Access information: lockbox instructions, door codes, smart-lock credentials, key handoff details, entry notes, and related access constraints you provide to enable service delivery.
- Business information (if applicable): company name, job title, and business contact details of authorized representatives.
- Payment-related information: we may receive payment confirmation, limited transaction details (e.g., transaction ID, status), and other limited payment information from our payment processors. We do not intentionally collect or store full card numbers; payments are processed by third-party payment providers.
- Communications: content of messages you send us via email, SMS/text, web forms, or phone.

B. Information collected automatically (website and devices)
- Device and usage data: IP address, browser type, device identifiers, pages visited, time spent, clicks, and referring URLs.
- Cookies and similar technologies: used to operate the website, remember preferences, analyze traffic, and (if enabled) measure advertising performance. See Section 7.

C. Information collected during service delivery
- Quality control photos ("QC photos"): before/after photos as described in Section 9 and our Terms.
- Call recordings (if used): where permitted by law and after appropriate notice/consent as described in Section 6.
- Service notes: internal notes about service completion, access issues, and scope-related observations relevant to performance and quality.

D. Information from third parties
- Scheduling/CRM and communications tools: appointment metadata, delivery status, opt-in/opt-out status, timestamps, and message routing information.
- Payment processors: payment confirmation and limited transaction details.
- Review platforms: information you submit publicly (e.g., Google, Yelp), and related engagement metadata.

9) Photos for Quality Control (QC)

Purpose. We may take before/after photos solely for internal quality control, training, and dispute review. We use reasonable efforts to avoid capturing people, personal documents, or sensitive items.

No marketing use without consent. We do not use QC photos for marketing, public posting, or advertising without your separate express consent.

Permitted disclosure. We may share QC photos with our insurers, legal counsel, and/or dispute-resolution providers as reasonably necessary to investigate, defend, or resolve a claim or dispute.

Retention. QC photos are typically retained for up to 90 days, unless longer retention is reasonably needed for a dispute or to comply with law.

Opt-out. You may opt out by notifying us before service begins (phone/SMS/email/message). If you opt out, we may request an alternative acceptance/verification process (e.g., walk-through, checklist sign-off), and the absence of photos may limit our ability to investigate certain disputes.
