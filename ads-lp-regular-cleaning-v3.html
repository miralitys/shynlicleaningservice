Is Regular Cleaning Right for You?

Regular cleaning is for homes that need consistent upkeep, not a one-time reset.

Regular cleaning is perfect if:

  • You want your home consistently clean on a schedule
  • You prefer the same trusted cleaner every visit
  • You want to stop rebooking every time
  • Your home is already in decent shape

Consider a different service if:

  • Home has not been cleaned in months: deep cleaning
  • You are moving in or out: move cleaning
  • You need a one-time deep reset: deep cleaning
