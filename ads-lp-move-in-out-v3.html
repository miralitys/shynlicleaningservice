Is Move-In/Out Cleaning Right for You?

This service is designed for empty or near-empty homes during a move.

Move cleaning is perfect if:

  • You are moving out and need to pass a landlord inspection
  • You want your full security deposit back
  • You are moving into a new place and want it spotless first
  • The previous tenants left the place less than ideal
  • You are a landlord preparing a unit for new tenants

Consider a different service if:

  • You are staying and want ongoing cleaning: regular cleaning
  • Your home needs a reset but you are not moving: deep cleaning
  • You only need a light touch-up: pricing options

Frequently Asked Questions

How soon can you clean?

We offer same-day and next-day availability in many areas. Send a quote request and we will confirm your earliest slot.

Does the home need to be empty?

Ideally yes. Empty homes let us clean inside closets, behind appliances, and reach every surface. We can work around remaining items if needed.

Will this pass my landlord inspection?

Move-out cleaning is designed around common landlord inspection areas: oven, fridge, closets, baseboards, bathrooms, floors, and more.

Do you handle move-ins too?

Yes. Move-in cleaning helps make a new home truly clean before unpacking, including kitchens, bathrooms, closets, and surfaces.
